Ingredients

  • 1 box of Captian Crunch
  • 1 giant spoon
  • 1 giant bowl
  • 1 gallon of milk

Cap'n Crunch

Directions

Cap'n Crunch is the top cereal in the northern hemisphere and will continue to be the best way to start your morning.

How-to

  1. Get your large bowl and spoon out and ready them to be used.
  2. Pour your Cap'n Crunch in the bowl to the top.
  3. Pour your milk until the cereal is almost all covered by milk.
  4. Enjoy!
